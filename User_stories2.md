## User Stories

## User Registration | Priority: High | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a new user, I want to create an account using email or social login so that I can place orders and save my details.

## User Login & Authentication | Priority: High | Estimate: 1 day

Description: As a registered user, I want to log in securely so that my account and order history are protected.

## Browse Restaurants | Priority: High | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to browse nearby restaurants so that I can see what food options are available to me.

## View Restaurant Menu | Priority: High | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to view a restaurantâ€™s menu with prices and images so that I can decide what to order.

## Search and Filter Food | Priority: Medium | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to search and filter food by category, price, or rating so that I can quickly find what I want.

## Shopping Cart Management | Priority: High | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to add, remove, and update food items in my cart so that I can finalise my order correctly.

## Place Order | Priority: High | Estimate: 1 day

Description: As a customer, I want to place an order and choose delivery or pickup so that I can receive my food conveniently.

## Online Payment | Priority: High | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to pay using a secure online payment method so that my transaction is fast and safe.

## Order Tracking | Priority: Medium | Estimate: 2 days

Description: As a customer, I want to track my order status in real time so that I know when my food will arrive.

## Restaurant Menu Management | Priority: Low | Estimate: 3 days

Description: As a restaurant owner, I want to add, update, or remove menu items so that customers always see accurate information.
